## Quick Start

<Callout type="info">
New to Showcase? Start with our [Installation Guide](/getting-started/installation) to get up and running in minutes.
</Callout>

Get started quickly with these essential commands:

<Tabs items={["Player Commands", "Admin Commands"]}>
  <Tab value="Player Commands">
    <CommandSyntax>/showcase item</CommandSyntax>
    Share your currently held item with all players.

    <CommandSyntax>/showcase inventory [player] [duration]</CommandSyntax>
    Share your entire inventory, optionally with a specific player and custom duration.

    <CommandSyntax>[item]</CommandSyntax>
    Type `[item]` in chat to quickly share your held item.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Admin Commands">
    <CommandSyntax>/admin-showcase item &lt;player&gt; [receiver]</CommandSyntax>
    Share any player's item as an administrator.

    <CommandSyntax>/showcase-manage reload</CommandSyntax>
    Reload the mod configuration without restarting.

    <CommandSyntax>/showcase-manage list</CommandSyntax>
    View all active shares on the server.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
