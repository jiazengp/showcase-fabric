# Sharing Inventories

Share complete inventories with other players for easy item display and organization review.

## Types of Inventory Sharing

### Full Inventory
Share your complete 36-slot player inventory.

```
/showcase inventory [receivers] [duration] [description]
```

### Hotbar Only
Share only your 9-slot hotbar items.

```
/showcase hotbar [receivers] [duration] [description]
```

### Ender Chest
Share your ender chest contents (27 slots).

```
/showcase enderchest [receivers] [duration] [description]
```

## Examples

```
/showcase inventory                 # Share with everyone
/showcase hotbar @a 5m "My setup"   # Share hotbar for 5 minutes
/showcase enderchest Bob 1h         # Share ender chest with Bob for 1 hour
```

## Chat Keywords

- `[inventory]` or `[inv]` - Share full inventory
- `[hotbar]` or `[hb]` - Share hotbar only
- `[ender_chest]` or `[ec]` - Share ender chest

**Example:** `Check out my [inventory]!`

## Permissions

- `showcase.command.inventory` - Share full inventory
- `showcase.command.hotbar` - Share hotbar
- `showcase.command.enderchest` - Share ender chest
